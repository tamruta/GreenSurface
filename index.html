Pack More Products Using Less Material

Use 16% less packaging material


Green Surface is a wholistic design, with details considered for manufacturing, shipping, point of sale, consumer use, handling, storage, and recycling. It gives you the ability to make environmentally-friendly decisions.

Here's some of our favorite features:

  • Removable corner for full product access.

  • Tear-out side door for easy, single product access.

  • Tear-out perforated holes to
    carry easily.

  • Stronger triangular form than conventional rectangular form.

  • Rounded corners translate to rectangular or rounded pallet.

Do you see the difference between rectangular and triangular designs?

When compared with conventional, rectangular design, Green Surface reduces packaging material consumption by 16% and storage volume by 8% for the same volume of product packaged. This is achieved by adopting a triangular form with rounded corners.‍
