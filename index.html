Durable, Practical and Convenient

That's the Design


Green Surface is a wholistic design, with details considered for manufacturing, shipping, point of sale, consumer use, handling, storage, and recycling. It gives you the ability to make environmentally-friendly decisions.

Here's some of our favorite features:

  • Removable corner for full product access.

  • Tear-out side door for easy, single product access.

  • Tear-out perforated holes to carry easily.

  • Single, paperboard material body.

  • Stronger triangular form than conventional rectangular form.

  • Rounded corners translate to rectangular or rounded pallet.

How is our design better?

When compared with conventional, rectangular design, Green Surface reduces packaging material consumption by 16% and storage volume by 8% for the same volume of product packaged. This is achieved by adopting a triangular form with rounded corners.‍Saves millions of trees.Less packaging material. Less cost on material, printing, handling, and storage.
